About us







We are government funded institute which comes under navodaya vidyalaya samiti. Our mission is to provide the quality education to ecomically poor students studying in rural areas. The school starts its registration from 6th class. We make sure that students studying here go through all-round development in their upbringing.Its a boarding school so a student go through a lot of activities in their day to day life. It also helps students to have discipline in their lives.

The school is equipped with various facilites such as play area, mess, auditorium, hostel facility, gym

students participate in daily activities like morning gathering for exercise, classes, remedial, shramdan and play
